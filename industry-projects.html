Industry Projects

Designing a Centralized
User Research Repository

Overview

Contributed to the design and rollout of a centralized, GDPR-compliant User Research Repository (URR) to improve access to user research across departments.

Problem

User research was siloed across teams, leading to duplicated efforts, limited cross-team insights, and missed opportunities for cross-product alignment.

My Role

  • Organized repository structure and information architecture to improve discoverability
  • Developed documentation and best-practice usage guidelines
  • Led and supported town hall demonstrations to introduce the URR
  • Helped onboard teams and drive cross-department adoption

Impact

Increased visibility of user insights across products, reduced redundant research efforts, enabled more user-centered, data-informed decision-making, and supported AI-assisted analyses for faster insight generation.
